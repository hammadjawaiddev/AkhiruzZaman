Prophetic Sequence

End Times Timeline

The sequence of major events as described in authenticated Islamic narrations

📜

The exact sequence of End Times events is a matter of scholarly discussion. This timeline presents the most widely accepted ordering based on authenticated narrations. Some events may overlap or occur in close succession. Allah ﷻ alone knows the precise timing of all these events.

Minor Signs Major Signs Key Events
Minor Signs

📖 The Spreading of Knowledge & Its Disappearance

According to hadith, religious knowledge will spread and then recede. Ignorance will become widespread, and the Quran will be lifted from the hearts of people — though its physical copies will remain.

Reference: Sahih Bukhari & Muslim

Minor Signs

🌍 Widespread Corruption & Fitnah

Great tribulations (fitna) will spread across the earth. People will be confused about right and wrong. The Prophet ﷺ warned that a believer's faith will melt like salt in water if they are not careful.

Reference: Sahih Muslim, Book of Tribulations

Minor Signs

🏗️ Competition in Building Tall Structures

The Prophet ﷺ mentioned that among the signs of the Hour is that you will see barefoot, naked, destitute shepherds competing in constructing tall buildings — a sign of reversed priorities and worldly competition.

Reference: Sahih Muslim (Hadith of Jibril)

Minor Signs

💰 Wealth & Strife Among Muslims

Wealth will become so abundant that it becomes difficult to find recipients for charity. Internal conflicts will arise among Muslims. The Prophet ﷺ warned particularly about the tribulation of wealth.

Reference: Sahih Bukhari

Major Sign

⚔ Malhama al-Kubra

The Great War — an unprecedented conflict involving great nations. Muslims, following the leadership of the Mahdi, will ultimately emerge victorious. This event closely precedes the emergence of the Dajjal.

Reference: Sahih Muslim — narrated by Abu Hurayrah (R.A)

Major Event

☪ Emergence of Imam Mahdi

The Mahdi appears — a righteous man from the family of the Prophet ﷺ, pledged allegiance near the Kaaba. He leads the Muslims, unites them, and establishes justice. He will lead prayers while Prophet Isa (A.S) prays behind him.

Reference: Sunan Abu Dawud, Sahih Muslim

Major Sign

👁 Emergence of Ad-Dajjal

The False Messiah emerges — the greatest trial in human history. He will roam the earth for forty days (one like a year, one like a month, one like a week, the rest normal days). He cannot enter Makkah or Madinah.

Reference: Sahih Muslim — narrated by al-Nawwas ibn Sam'an (R.A)

Major Event

✦ Descent of Prophet Isa (A.S)

Prophet Isa (Jesus, peace be upon him) descends near the white minaret east of Damascus. He joins the Muslims led by the Mahdi, prays behind him, then pursues and kills the Dajjal at the gate of Ludd.

Reference: Sahih Muslim, Sahih Bukhari

Major Sign

🌊 Release of Ya'juj and Ma'juj (Gog & Magog)

After the Dajjal is defeated, Ya'juj and Ma'juj (Gog and Magog) will be released — vast multitudes who will cause great destruction. Prophet Isa (A.S) and the believers will seek refuge, and Allah ﷻ will send a plague that destroys them.

Reference: Sahih Muslim — narrated by al-Nawwas ibn Sam'an (R.A)

Era of Peace

🕊 Era of Justice Under Isa (A.S)

After the defeat of the Dajjal and Ya'juj Ma'juj, an era of extraordinary peace, justice, and abundance will prevail. Isa (A.S) will rule justly for approximately forty years, then pass away. Muslims will pray his funeral prayer.

Reference: Sunan Abu Dawud, narrated by Abu Hurayrah (R.A)

Major Sign

🌫 Ad-Dukhan — The Smoke

A great smoke will cover the earth. It will afflict the disbelievers severely (like intoxication) and cause mild discomfort to the believers (like a cold). This is mentioned in Surah Ad-Dukhan (44:10-11).

Reference: Surah Ad-Dukhan (44:10); Sahih Muslim

Major Sign

🌅 Rising of the Sun from the West

The sun will rise from the west — after which repentance will no longer be accepted. The Prophet ﷺ mentioned this as one of the clearest signs after which the gates of repentance close permanently.

Reference: Sahih Bukhari & Muslim

Major Sign

🦎 The Beast of the Earth (Dabbat al-Ard)

A great beast will emerge from the earth, speaking to people and marking the believers and disbelievers. This is mentioned in Surah An-Naml (27:82): "And when the Word has been fulfilled against them, We will bring forth for them a creature from the earth speaking to them…"

Reference: Surah An-Naml (27:82); Sahih Muslim

Final Major Sign

🔥 The Great Fire from Yemen

A fire will emerge from the direction of Yemen (or Aden), driving all people to the place of their gathering — the final assembly before the Day of Judgement. This is the last of the ten major signs.

Reference: Sahih Muslim — narrated by Hudhayfah ibn Asid (R.A)

The Final Hour

☀ Yawm al-Qiyamah — The Day of Judgement

The Hour arrives. The trumpet (Sur) will be blown by Israfil (A.S). All creation will perish, then be resurrected for the final accounting before Allah ﷻ. Every soul will receive its full recompense for its deeds.

Reference: Surah Az-Zumar (39:68); Surah Al-Haqqah

"Hasten to perform good deeds before six things occur: the rising of the sun from the west, the Smoke, the Dajjal, the Beast, (the death of) one of you, or general calamity."

Sahih Muslim — narrated by Abu Hurayrah (R.A)
