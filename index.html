Understanding the End of Times

According to Islamic teachings, the world will experience a series of major events before the Day of Judgement (Yawm al-Qiyamah). These events have been described in detail by the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ through authenticated narrations preserved in the great hadith collections.

The knowledge of these signs is meant to strengthen our faith, inspire preparation through righteous deeds, and remind believers of the ultimate accountability before Allah ﷻ.

This website presents these teachings in an educational and respectful manner, drawing exclusively from the Quran and authentic hadith sources, without sectarian bias or speculation about current events.
