Topic 02 — Major Signs

Al-Masih ad-Dajjal

The False Messiah — The Greatest Trial for Humanity

⚠️

The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ warned of the Dajjal as the greatest trial (fitnah) humanity will ever face. This page presents his descriptions from authenticated Hadith for educational and protective awareness. We seek refuge in Allah from his trial.

The Greatest Trial

According to Islamic teachings, Al-Masih ad-Dajjal (the False Messiah, or Antichrist) represents the greatest tribulation in human history. The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ warned about him so frequently and emphatically that his companions noted it was one of the subjects he spoke about most in his sermons.

"There has been no prophet between Adam and me, but he warned his nation about the one-eyed liar (Dajjal). Beware! He is one-eyed, and your Lord is not one-eyed. Written between his eyes is 'Kafir' (disbeliever)."

Sahih Bukhari, Book of Tribulations

Physical Description

The Prophet ﷺ described the Dajjal's appearance in considerable detail across multiple narrations, so that believers would recognize him:

1

He will be one-eyed (blind in the right eye), described as if the eye is a floating grape.

2

The word "Kafir" (كافر) will be written between his eyes, visible to every believer, literate or not.

3

He will be a young man with reddish complexion, short and stout, with thick curly hair.

4

He will be sterile — he will leave no offspring behind him.

الدَّجَّالُ أَعْوَرُ الْعَيْنِ الْيُسْرَى جُفَالٌ الشَّعَرِ مَعَهُ جَنَّةٌ وَنَارٌ

"The Dajjal is blind in his left eye (or right in other narrations), has thick curly hair, and with him will be what appears to be Paradise and Fire…"

Sahih Muslim, narrated by Hudhayfah (R.A)

His Powers and Deceptions

According to hadith, the Dajjal will be granted extraordinary abilities by Allah ﷻ as a test for humanity. These abilities will be used to deceive people into following him:

  • He will command the skies to rain, and it will rain; he will command the earth to produce, and it will produce crops
  • He will appear to bring the dead back to life (in reality, these will be devils in human form)
  • He will travel the earth at tremendous speed
  • With him will be what appears to be paradise and hellfire — in reality, what appears as paradise is fire, and what appears as fire is cool water
  • He will claim to be God — this is the greatest lie in human history

"He will have with him fire and water. What the people think is fire will be cool water, and what they think is water will be fire. So whoever among you encounters that (time) should fall into what he thinks is fire, for it will be cool and pleasant."

Sahih Bukhari & Sahih Muslim

Where He Will and Will Not Go

According to authentic narrations, the Dajjal will travel throughout the earth spreading his deception. However, there are sacred places he will be unable to enter:

✓

Makkah al-Mukarramah — Angels will guard the entrances; the Dajjal will be unable to enter.

✓

Madinah al-Munawwarah — It will be shaken with three tremors that will expel the hypocrites; the Dajjal cannot enter.

✓

Masjid al-Aqsa (Baitul Maqdis) — The Dajjal will surround it but will be prevented from entering.

✗

Rest of the earth — He will traverse all other lands during his period of forty days.

Duration of His Appearance

"We said: O Messenger of Allah, how long will he stay on earth? He said: Forty days — one day like a year, one day like a month, one day like a week, and the rest of his days like your days."

Sahih Muslim, narrated by al-Nawwas ibn Sam'an (R.A)

This means his total period will be the equivalent of approximately 14 months and 18 days in normal time, though scholars have differed on the precise calculation.

Protection from the Dajjal's Trial

The Prophet ﷺ provided specific guidance on how believers can protect themselves from the Dajjal's deception. This is of great importance for every Muslim:

🛡️
Recite the first 10 verses of Surah Al-Kahf The Prophet ﷺ said: "Whoever among you encounters him (the Dajjal), let him recite over him the opening verses of Surah Al-Kahf." — Sahih Muslim
  • Seek refuge in Allah from the trial of the Dajjal — especially in the final tashahhud of each prayer
  • Memorise and recite the first ten verses of Surah Al-Kahf
  • Flee from him — go to the mountains if necessary
  • Remember that no believer who truly knows Allah will be deceived; the Dajjal cannot claim to be Allah when every Muslim knows that Allah has no defects

His End

According to Islamic narrations, the Dajjal's reign of terror will come to an end at the hands of Prophet Isa (Jesus, peace be upon him) after his descent from the heavens. The Dajjal will flee from Isa (A.S) and will ultimately be killed at the gate of Ludd (Lod) in the region of present-day Palestine.

"The son of Maryam will kill the Dajjal at the gate of Ludd."

Sunan al-Tirmidhi — Classified Sahih
